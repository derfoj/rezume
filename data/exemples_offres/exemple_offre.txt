Full job description
Context:

Following a collaborative project cofounded by the EU, HERON, which aims to reduce the CO2 emission of air traffic and to develop sustainable activities on the ground and in the air, we offer an internship in optimization of events on airport surface.

You will be joining the “Airport & Monitoring” team, and you will take part in the development of new video analysis functions. The goal is to incorporate them into our supervision solution “airside”.

Goal:

Advance our event management product of airport surface « SAGA », including machine learning and video stream processing technologies.

Activity:

You will be working in an « agile » environment, and your missions will be:

· To enhance and improve the reliability of our “Deep Learning” learning process.

· To automate camera calibration, detect and alert changes (breakdown, camera motion, obstruction…).

· To take part in validation campaigns in airports.

· To include the developed units into our airport management product “SAGA”.

· Development using Java, C++ and Python.

· To contribute to the writing of the technical documentation.

Knowledge that will be improved during the internship:

· Real time supervision’s tool

· Machine learning

· Video stream processing

· Web development

Your profile:

· In the last year of a training level “BAC +5” (Engineer school or university education)

· Taste for software development Java, C++, Python.

· Appreciated knowledge:

- Machine Learning

- Video stream processing

· Good level of technical English

Conditions:

Internship, 6 months long, gross salary (1100to 1400 € /month depending on schooling level), luncheon voucher[GM1] and public transport refund for 75%.

Who are we:

We are an innovative and expert company, which conceives, develops and commercializes products and solutions for airport and air traffic management, selling all over the world to the major aeronautics players.

Based in Toulouse, in the city centre, close to “Canal du Midi” (easy access subway, bus, bicycle and train).

Joining ALTYS Technologies means:

· To be part of a team, expert and leading in its field.

· To work in a small engineer team, allowing autonomy, initiatives, quick decisions and recognition.

· To become an actor of technical choices and development of our products from conception to delivery.

· To be involved with innovative and future technologies.

· To have various assignments.

· To evolve in an international situation.

· To take part in the diffusion of our values which are respect, trust, listening and dialogue.
